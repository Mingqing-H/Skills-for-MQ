# Image Prompt Patterns

Use professional, varied prompts. Do not default to anime or cartoon.

## Prompt Components

Always include:

- subject
- composition
- visual style
- color palette
- lighting
- aspect ratio
- text strategy
- negative constraints

## Cover Prompt

`High-end editorial cover for a Chinese WeChat technology essay about [topic]. Main subject: [visual metaphor]. Composition: strong central focal point, readable negative space for title, layered depth. Style: [magazine photography / cinematic tech poster / 3D editorial / minimalist vector / ink-wash tech fusion]. Palette: [specific palette]. Lighting: [specific lighting]. Aspect ratio 16:9, also suitable for 1:1 crop. No real company logos, no fake UI, no small unreadable text.`

## Professional Styles

- **Tech magazine poster**: clean geometry, realistic devices, deep shadows, cyan/teal accents.
- **Editorial photography**: realistic lighting, shallow depth of field, natural texture.
- **3D concept diagram**: isometric objects, labeled zones added later, no baked-in text.
- **Minimal infographic**: flat shapes, restrained palette, clear hierarchy, data added later.
- **Ink-wash technology**: Chinese ink texture plus subtle circuit/AI motifs.
- **Fashion poster**: high contrast, stylish composition, bold negative space.
- **Editorial cartoon**: refined linework, metaphorical scene, not childish.

## Chart Prompt

For generated chart-like visuals, use only as a background or concept unless data is supplied. Prefer generating a clean frame and adding real chart data separately.

`Professional data visualization background for [topic], abstract line chart and bar chart motifs, no exact numbers, no fake labels, clean grid, editorial tech style, high resolution, 16:9.`
