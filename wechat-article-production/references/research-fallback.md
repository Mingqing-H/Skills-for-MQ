# Research Fallback

Use this when specialized AI-news skills are unavailable or insufficient.

## Source Priority

1. Official company pages, product docs, blogs, release notes, pricing pages.
2. Government/regulator pages, court filings, standards bodies.
3. Papers, technical reports, benchmark repos, model/system cards.
4. Reliable media with dates and named sources.
5. Social posts only as sentiment or primary-person statements; verify elsewhere when possible.

## Research Pack Format

For each source, record:

- `source`: publisher or organization
- `title`
- `url`
- `date`: publication/update date
- `claim_supported`
- `use_in_article`: fact, quote, image, background, or counterpoint

## Handling Uncertainty

- If a source conflicts, state the conflict in notes and use cautious wording.
- If a claim cannot be verified, write "有报道/传闻/市场讨论称" only when you cite the reporting context.
- Avoid "最新", "已经", "首次", "确定" unless the source supports it.
- Prefer exact dates over relative time.

## 5-Industry Case Selection

For "AI reshaping industries" style topics:

- Define "landing case" before selecting examples.
- Collect more than 5 candidate industries.
- Score each candidate by evidence strength, recency, reader relevance, workflow change, and visual material availability.
- Select the strongest 5; do not preselect industries.
